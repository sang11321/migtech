# MIGTECH Website Design Guidelines

## Design Approach
**Professional B2B Industrial with Portfolio Showcase** - Drawing inspiration from premium industrial/technology companies (Siemens, ABB, GE) combined with modern agency portfolio aesthetics. The design emphasizes technical credibility, large-scale project capabilities, and professional trust-building for B2B partners.

## Core Design Elements

### A. Typography
- **Headings**: Inter or Poppins (600-700 weight) - clean, modern, professional
- **Body**: Inter or Roboto (400-500 weight) for excellent Vietnamese character rendering
- **Scale**: 
  - Hero headline: 3.5rem (desktop) / 2rem (mobile)
  - Section headers: 2.5rem / 1.75rem
  - Subsections: 1.5rem / 1.25rem  
  - Body: 1rem / 0.95rem
  - Small text: 0.875rem

### B. Layout System
- **Container**: max-w-7xl with px-4/px-6 padding
- **Spacing units**: Tailwind 4, 6, 8, 12, 16, 20, 24 for consistent rhythm
- **Section padding**: py-16/py-20 (desktop), py-12/py-16 (mobile)
- **Grid**: 3-column for services, 2-column for projects, masonry for photo gallery

### C. Component Library

**Navigation**
- Sticky header with logo left, menu items right
- Transparent over hero, solid white with shadow on scroll
- Bilingual menu: Trang Chủ | Giới Thiệu | Dự Án | Dịch Vụ | Liên Hệ
- Mobile: Hamburger menu with slide-in drawer

**Hero Section**
- Full-width, 85vh height with Landmark 81 nighttime LED lighting image
- Overlay gradient (dark to transparent top-to-bottom)
- Centered content: Company tagline + primary CTA with blur background
- Subtle downward scroll indicator

**Service Cards**
- 4 cards in grid (2x2 on tablet, 1 column mobile)
- Icon at top, service name, short description, "Xem thêu" link
- Subtle border, hover: slight elevation and border color change
- Icons: LED bulb, circuit board, design tools, shipping box

**Project Showcase**
- Masonry grid layout for project photos
- Overlay on hover: project name, client, brief description
- Lightbox gallery for full-size viewing
- Filter tabs: Tất cả | LED Mặt Dựng | Thiết Bị | Thiết Kế

**Partner Logos**
- Grayscale logos in row, centered
- Hover: color restoration with smooth transition
- GS, Vinpearl, Vinhomes prominently displayed

**Contact Section**
- Split layout: Form (left 60%) + Company info with map (right 40%)
- Form fields: Tên, Email, Điện thoại, Nội dung
- Company details: Address, phone, email with icons

**Footer**
- Multi-column: About | Services | Projects | Contact
- Social media icons (Zalo, phone)
- Copyright and company registration info

### D. Animations
- Minimal: Smooth scroll to sections, subtle hover states only
- Fade-in on scroll for sections (intersection observer)
- No distracting parallax or complex animations

## Images

### Required Images:

1. **Hero Image** (1920x1080px minimum)
   - Landmark 81 at night with LED facade lighting fully visible
   - Dramatic angle showing building's height and LED installation
   - High quality, professional architectural photography

2. **Service Section Icons** (4 images)
   - Professional icon graphics or photos representing each service
   - Consistent style (line icons or isometric illustrations)

3. **Project Gallery** (8-12 images minimum)
   - Landmark 81 installation process and results (4-5 images)
   - Battery testing equipment design (3-4 images)  
   - Team working on-site in safety gear (2-3 images)
   - Close-ups of LED installations and electronic work

4. **Partner Logos** (3 images)
   - GS, Vinpearl, Vinhomes logos in high resolution
   - PNG format with transparency

5. **Company/Team Photo** (1 image)
   - Professional team photo or company facility
   - For "About" section credibility

## Color Strategy
While colors will be defined later, the design structure accommodates:
- Primary brand color (from MIGTECH logo - red/orange tones)
- Professional neutrals for body and backgrounds
- Accent color for CTAs and highlights
- Dark overlay for hero text legibility

## Key Design Principles
1. **Professional Credibility**: Clean, organized layouts that convey technical expertise
2. **Visual Impact**: Large, high-quality project images showcase capabilities
3. **Clear Hierarchy**: Easy navigation between services, projects, and contact
4. **B2B Focus**: Information-dense sections with clear value propositions
5. **Vietnamese-First**: All content in Vietnamese with proper typography support
6. **Mobile Excellence**: Fully responsive with touch-friendly interactions
